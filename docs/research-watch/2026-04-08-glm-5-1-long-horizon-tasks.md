## Why this is worth watching
GLM-5.1 is ZhipuAI's latest model explicitly framed around "long-horizon tasks" — multi-step agentic workflows that require sustained reasoning across many turns. This is a direct competitor to frontier models for the class of agent tasks where current LLMs degrade over long context. ## Why clawfit should care
Long-horizon capability is a missing dimension in clawfit's current LLM registry. The `latency: high` budget profiles (research loops, data-analysis pipelines) are exactly where GLM-5.1 positions itself. If the model delivers on its framing, it should be added to the LLM registry as an alternative to GPT-4o/Claude for `task: research` + `frequency: daily` profiles. Also signals that "task duration" or "context horizon" may need to become a scoring axis.
